ABOUT

A husband. A self taught designer and developer.

I was a former graphic designer with the startup company. Most my work and experience is digital and print design.

I started my transition from graphic designer to web developer/web designer last February when a friend asked me to help him to his startup agency. After that i focus on web development watching tutorials in youtube about wordpress and elementor, then teaching myself html and css to know the fundamentals of web development. Im struggling especially in Javascript(still trying to grasp it). Still learning and always finding my motivation to not give up on my dreams to become a developer.

SKILLS: HTML, CSS and Javascript, Wordpress, Elementor and Divi.
TOOLS: Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, XD and Figma.
